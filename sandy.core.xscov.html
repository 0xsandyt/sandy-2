sandy.core.xscov module

class sandy.core.xscov.XsCov(*args, **kwargs)

Bases: sandy.core.basecov.BaseCov

Dataframe to contain cross section/nubar covariance matrices. Covariances can be stored for:

  • individual reactions,

  • cross reactions,

  • cross isotopes,

  • cross sections vs nubar

Index:

  • MAT : (int) MAT number to identify the isotope

  • MT : (int) MT number to identify the reaction

  • E : (float) energy of the incident particle

Columns:

  • MAT : (int) MAT number to identify the isotope

  • MT : (int) MT number to identify the reaction

  • E : (float) energy of the incident particle

Values: matrix coefficients
